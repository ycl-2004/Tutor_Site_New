Differential Calculus

This course covers the fundamentals of differential calculus, including limits, derivatives, and their applications. Topics include the language of limits, continuity, tangent lines, rules of differentiation, implicit and logarithmic differentiation, related rates, curve sketching, optimization, and an introduction to multivariable calculus.
